Instructions

  • Click on the circle to add a new note.
  • Drag a note around to change it's pitch
  • To remove a note, drag it to the edge of the screen
  • Use the hotkeys above to play with the arrangements
  • Hit the record button to start saving a wave file of your performance
  • To stop recording, click the record button once more.
